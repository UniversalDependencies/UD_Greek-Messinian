# Summary

The file contains annotated material of the Messenian dialect of Modern Greek. Messenian belongs to the Southern Group of Modern Greek dialects and shares a lot of features with the main variety of Modern Greek (the Standard).

# Introduction

This file contains transcribed oral material (one hour and ten minutes) from internviews with native speakers of the Messenian dialect of Modern Greek. The material was collected in 2023. Interviews were collected from six women (22,24, 27, 49, 60 years old) and two men (52, 86 years old). Transcription was performed with Whisper large – v3 without fine tuning and were edited manually by a native speaker of the dialect.

Annotation was performed with cycles of knowledge transfer from UD_GUD (treebank of the Standard) and manual editing.
